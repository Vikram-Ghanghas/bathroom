Service description

The restoration of wooden floors should be considered long before it finally falls into disrepair. Otherwise, there may be serious difficulties with the return of the original form. Restoration work should be started immediately after the floor covering begins to form scratches, cracks, and creaking. Thanks to the restoration of wooden floors, it is possible to quickly restore their beautiful appearance, it is also possible to change the color to any desired one by toning the parquet or applying a color toning composition. Such work is preferable to a complete replacement of the floor covering.
